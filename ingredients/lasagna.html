ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef
  • Lasagna

Steps

  1. Cook beef
  2. Mix tomato sauce in beef and cook both together
  3. Boil lasagna until soft
  4. Place the newly made sauce on top of the lasagna then place more lasagna on top and repeat it in layers
  5. Sprinkle cheese ontop
  6. Heat for 1 hour on low
  7. Enjoy!
